Our Mission

Our goal is to combine education and technology to create a fun and engaging learning experience for the next generation. Through our games 'Cable Ranger' and 'Spectra' we encourage kids to learn about internet infrastructure and color spectrums in an interactive and exciting environment.

Cable Ranger

Previous Next

Fix the Internet!

YOU have the chance to rewire your city by learning how to find and fix the hidden systems which link the city's internet networks together. You'll learn about where the internet actually comes from, identifying and reconnecting data centers, cell towers, public wifi routers and distributed antenna systems that exist above ground. You'll also get to venture down below, learning to understand the secret system of symbols that lets you navigate the underground cable networks.

Spectra

Color Battles!

Spectra is a fast paced action game where you must battle colorful enemy spaceships that may only be hit with the right color. Equipped with blue, yellow, and red bullets, you must shoot through the correct color filter to match the color of the enemy. Shoot red through blue to hit purple enemies and yellow through green to hit green ones. Learn about color spectrums while saving the universe!
